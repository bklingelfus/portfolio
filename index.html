bio

Hello! My name is Bernardo Klingelfus and I'm a software engineer based in Brazil. I love to solve problems and think creatively about solutions as well as turning ideas into reality, so that's what got me into coding!

background

Having two bachelors, one in Business Administration and another in Economics, I was always very interested in businesses, but from the very beginning of my degrees international/global opportunities sparked my interest, since for me those ae usually associate with what is the most modern. Pursuing positions related to that matter, I learned a lot about the macro side of those operations and realized that if I wanted to work with jobs that shape the future and have potential to be global, I had to get into the tech industry.

interests

As I mentioned previously, I really enjoy working with things that excite me about the future. So working with tech products is something that I have found meets that very well, specially when taking into account other areas that I like to work, whether it is the international scenario or being involved with different experiences for myself and customers.

hobbies

One of the things that I really appreciate is the power of story telling. Whether it is in the professional world or for entertainment, I believe there is a big difference in the impact of the message that you want to convey when you do it in the right way.

So, of course I do like movies and series a lot, a proper one can be a hell of a ride! On that note, I also play video games, most of the time solo open world games, but everyone has that one guilty pleasure (I'm talking about you LoL). And I am also ver into sports, there are a lot of amazing stories that could have been just movies but are in fact reality. Some of my favorites are footbal, american footbal, basketball, tennis and baseball.

experiences

General Assembly

At GA, I was able to connect with the tech world in a more practical manner, working on different projects to develop and enhance many skills as a developer. This experience helped me understand how the industry works and acquired essential abilities for having a successful career.

Core Skills:

  • programming/coding skills;
  • project management;
  • industry knowledge.

ExxonMobil

During my time at ExxonMobil, I was part of the Global Planning team, an extremely concise group that at the same time was very internationally spread out, present in 5 different locations in 4 continents.

There, I acquired some essential skills for understanding how big corporations operate and how to get the most of the company resources. Being part of the Global Planning team meant we were at the center of the company's business intelligence area, which in turn involved analysing and interacting with different subjects on a macro level.

Core Skills:

  • business intelligence (BI);
  • data analytics;
  • high level communication.

AIESEC

AIESEC is the biggest moviment of young leadership in the world. Made up of a international platform that enables personal and professional development for people under 30 by providing multicultural exchange experiences that incentivate leadership and teamwork.

In the organization, I was able to be part of the leadership board two times and in those instances I learned two crucial abilities. The first one is how to be a team player both as a manager and as a member by participating in highly diverse background groups. The second is understanding the product and how it connects with the costumer, since the client experience is the core of any company.

Core Skills:

  • team management;
  • costumer experience;
  • team player.
